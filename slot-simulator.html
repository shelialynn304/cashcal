What RTP can and cannot tell you

RTP is the long-run average return over very large samples. It does not forecast what your next 100 or 500 spins will do.

Use this simulator to compare equal bankrolls at 94% vs 96.5% RTP and observe how short-run variance dominates session outcomes.

Volatility vs bankroll pain

Higher volatility often means more missed spins in a row, larger drawdowns, and occasional spikes. Your bankroll needs to survive the drought.
