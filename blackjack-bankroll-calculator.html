How much bankroll do you need?

More than most people bring. If you are betting too large a chunk of your bankroll each hand, short-term variance can wreck you fast even if the game edge is relatively low.

Smaller bets buy survival. Bigger bets buy stress and a shorter story.

What this does not mean

A decent simulation result does not mean you have an advantage. It means you have a range of possible short-term outcomes inside a negative-expectation game.
