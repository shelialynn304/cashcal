About this blackjack expected value calculator

This blackjack expected value (EV) calculator helps you compare the long-term profitability of different decisions, including hit, stand, and double. By entering your hand, the dealer’s upcard, and basic table conditions, you can see which move has the highest expected return over time. Instead of guessing or relying on instinct, this tool gives you a data-driven edge by showing how each decision performs mathematically. It’s designed for both beginners learning basic strategy and experienced players who want to better understand the impact of their choices at the table.
