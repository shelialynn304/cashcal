Biggest mistakes slot players make

  • Increasing bet size after losses because the machine feels “due.”
  • Confusing bonus round excitement with positive expected value.
  • Ignoring how quickly total wager compounds over hundreds of spins.
  • Playing high volatility with a bankroll sized for low volatility pain.

Common myths about beating slots

“A cold machine must pay soon.”

No. Each spin is independent RNG.

“High RTP means I should win tonight.”

No. RTP is a long-run average over huge spin volumes.

“Bonuses guarantee profit.”

No. Bonus features are usually part of the same negative-EV math model.
