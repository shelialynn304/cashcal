Beginner interpretation guide

  • Win chance = how often your bet is expected to hit.
  • Payout = profit multiple on wins (stake is returned separately on real tables).
  • EV/spin = average gain/loss per spin over long samples.
  • Bust risk = simulated chance bankroll drops below your bet size before planned spins end.

Even high-hit bets still lose slowly over time due to built-in edge.

Roulette calculator FAQ

Why does expected value stay negative for all standard bets?

Payouts are slightly lower than true fair odds, and the zero pockets create that gap. This built-in difference is the house edge.

Can this tool predict exact future outcomes?

No. It models probabilities and long-run expectation. Individual sessions can deviate significantly due to variance.

Should I increase stake after losses?

Increasing stake raises volatility and bust risk. It can change short-term paths but not long-run expected value.
